# Prospect Research Workflow

Use this workflow manually until an account and sending channel are confirmed.
Do not spam broad lists. The first target is 30 highly relevant prospects.

## Search Queries

- `"OpenAI" "support bot" "YC" founder`
- `"Anthropic" "AI agent" "CTO" startup`
- `"LiteLLM" "we built" startup`
- `"LangChain" "case study" SaaS CTO`
- `"RAG" "customer support" founder`
- `site:github.com "OPENAI_API_KEY" "pricing" "SaaS"`

## Qualification Sheet Columns

- Company
- Contact name
- Role
- Why they likely spend on AI APIs
- Public signal URL
- Contact channel
- Message sent date
- Response
- Payment status

## Daily Quota

- Research 15 prospects
- Send 10 tailored messages
- Follow up after 3 business days
- Stop any thread immediately if they decline
